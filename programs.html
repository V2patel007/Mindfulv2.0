Preschool Program

18 months - 4 years

Our Preschool Program is where confidence grows. Here, children are encouraged to ask questions, express ideas, and solve problems—all through purposeful play and hands-on learning. We prepare children not only for kindergarten, but for life.

From science experiments to storytelling circles, our curriculum sparks curiosity and builds foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and emotional intelligence. Most importantly, children develop a strong sense of self and belonging that stays with them long after they leave our care.

✨ What parents can expect:

  • School readiness in a pressure-free, joyful environment

  • Development of social-emotional skills, empathy, and teamwork

  • Daily outdoor play and movement-based learning

  • Encouragement of creativity, critical thinking, and confidence
